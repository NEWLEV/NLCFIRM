Community Health Center Achieves Zero HIPAA Audit Findings

Multi-site community health center · Growth Retainer · 18-month engagement

The Challenge

A 4-site community health center had failed two consecutive external HIPAA audits with 32% of items flagged as non-compliant. Staff were overwhelmed, policies were outdated, and there was no formal risk assessment or breach notification process. The organization faced potential penalties of up to $1.5M.

Our Approach

  • Comprehensive HIPAA gap analysis across all 4 sites
  • Rebuilt all 47 policies and procedures from scratch
  • Implemented a formal risk assessment methodology
  • Trained 120+ staff members on HIPAA fundamentals
  • Deployed NLC monitoring dashboard for ongoing compliance tracking
  • Established breach notification SOPs and incident response protocols

The Outcome

Within 12 months, the organization passed its first external HIPAA audit with zero findings — a result they had never achieved in their 20-year history. The compliance program is now self-sustaining with NLC providing quarterly reviews.

"Zero findings on our first external audit — that had never happened before. NLC didn't just fix our compliance, they built a program that sustains itself."
— Rachel M., COO

💻 Telehealth

AI Billing Automation Saves $127K Annually

Telehealth practice · Growth Retainer + AI Project · 8-month engagement

The Challenge

A growing telehealth practice with 15 providers was spending 40+ hours per week on manual billing tasks. Claim denial rates were at 18% (industry average: 5-10%), and the billing team of 3 was struggling to keep up with volume that had tripled during the post-pandemic telehealth boom.

Our Approach

  • Audited the full revenue cycle from scheduling to payment
  • Identified 23 specific automation opportunities
  • Deployed AI-powered claims scrubbing before submission
  • Automated patient eligibility verification
  • Built custom reporting dashboard for real-time revenue metrics
  • Re-trained billing staff on exception-based workflows

The Outcome

The practice saved 30+ hours per month in manual billing work, reduced claim denials from 18% to 3.2%, and achieved $127K in annual savings through reduced labor costs and improved first-pass resolution rates. The ROI from the first quarter alone justified the entire year's retainer.

"The ROI in the first quarter justified the entire year's retainer. Our billing team went from firefighting to strategic oversight."
— Dr. David T., Founder

Within 24 months, the organization secured $2.1M in new grant funding, saw a 40% increase in operational efficiency, and transitioned from survival mode to strategic growth. The executive director now spends 70% of time on mission work instead of back-office tasks.
